About InBalance Therapies

InBalance Therapies is a professional mental health service offering counselling, psychotherapy, somatic approaches, and equine-facilitated therapy.

Each service is delivered with a trauma-informed, nervous-system aware approach, creating a safe, structured environment where individuals, teens, and groups can reconnect with themselves and find clarity, calm, and confidence.

InBalance Therapies focuses on experiential, body-based, and relational methods that support individuals to integrate real change.
